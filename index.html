How we met

As of writing this story, Ruochen and Eric have known each other for a year. But when they look back at their time together, it feels so much longer than that.

There were many opportunities Ruochen and Eric could have met each other earlier. Starting 11 years ago when Ruochen landed at O'Hare airport, Eric could have tried to bump into her and given her his number. But he missed. And when Eric graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, he should have tried to find a job near San Francisco to wait for Ruochen's return. But he went to the other side of the country and spent too much time on the East Coast. And it was too much time indeed, since Ruochen started studying at the University of Chicago a year before Eric would even return.

As the saying goes, it's better late than never. But Ruochen and Eric never felt that way. They met on a cold December night at Beatnik on the River. And they felt a connection that they have not felt with anyone else, like they were always looking for each other. Ruochen mentioned she wanted to see Star Wars. So seeing the opportunity to see Ruochen again, Eric aksed if Ruochen wanted to see the movie with him the next day. And she agreed! From that weekend, they saw each other every week. And what was a weekly visit turned to a daily visit. And what was a daily visit turned to living with each other. And now they are getting married!

Ruochen and Eric spent too much time missing each other the past 11 years. So all they can do now is to love each other as much as they can and dedicate themselves to the other. And they welcome you to witness this beautiful wedding as they make this new chapter in their lives together!
